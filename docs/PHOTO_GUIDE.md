## ðŸ› ï¸ Image Optimization Guidelines

### Before Uploading

1. **Resize to specified dimensions**
   - Don't upload oversized images
   - Use exact dimensions listed above
   - Maintain aspect ratios

2. **Compress images**
   - Use TinyPNG.com or Squoosh.app
   - Target: <300KB for large images, <150KB for medium
   - Quality: 85-90% for JPG, default for WebP

3. **Convert to WebP**
   - Modern format, better compression
   - Keep JPG/PNG as fallback
   - Tools: Squoosh, Cloudconvert, Photoshop

4. **Descriptive file names**
   - Use hyphens, not spaces
   - Include relevant keywords
   - Example: `coffee-bar-premium-seating.webp`

## ðŸ“ Photo Rights & Permissions

### Legal Considerations

1. **Ownership:** Ensure PJ's Auto Care owns rights to all photos
2. **Photographer release:** Get written permission from photographer
3. **People in photos:** Obtain signed photo releases from:
   - Staff members
   - Customers (if visible and identifiable)
4. **Trademarked logos:** Use official brand assets from manufacturers

### Recommended Approach

1. **Hire professional photographer**
   - Commercial photography package
   - Includes full rights transfer
   - Usually 2-4 hours on-site
   - Estimate: $500-$1,500

2. **DIY Photography Tips**
   - Use good smartphone camera (12+ megapixels)
   - Shoot in natural daylight
   - Avoid motion blur
   - Multiple angles of each subject
   - Clean/stage areas before shooting
